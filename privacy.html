Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

TabbiMate ("TabbiMate," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

When you use TabbiMate, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and basic profile information
  • Language preferences and learning level
  • Messages, audio, or video content you choose to share during conversations
  • Feedback or support requests you submit

b. Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data (pages visited, session duration, interactions)

c. Audio & Video Conversations

TabbiMate may process audio and video streams to:

  • Facilitate live conversations
  • Provide AI-powered feedback, corrections, or suggestions

We do not sell your audio or video data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Service
  • Match you with language mates
  • Improve conversation quality using AI assistance
  • Analyze usage to improve features
  • Communicate with you about updates or support
  • Maintain safety, security, and legal compliance

3. AI Processing

TabbiMate uses AI to:

  • Assist conversations in real time
  • Analyze language usage and mistakes
  • Suggest improvements or next responses

AI outputs are assistance only and should not be treated as professional advice.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share information:

  • With service providers (hosting, analytics, video infrastructure)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect users, the Service, or our rights

We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the Service
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Improve product quality

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or correct your data
  • Request deletion
  • Object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@tabbimate.com.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure.

8. Children

TabbiMate is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service means you accept the updated policy.
